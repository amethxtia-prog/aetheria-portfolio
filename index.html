Aetheria Studio

Designing ethereal experiences grounded in real-world impact.

Aetheria Studio is a multidisciplinary design practice exploring the intersection of nature, storytelling, and human wellbeing — transforming concepts into spatial, visual, and material systems.

A designer balancing emotion and system.

My work exists between the poetic and the precise. I design systems that feel soft and immersive, yet are grounded in research, intentionality, and real-world impact. I am especially drawn to projects that explore sustainability, wellbeing, and human-centered experiences — translating abstract concepts into tangible, meaningful design outcomes.
