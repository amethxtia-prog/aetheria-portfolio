About

A designer balancing emotion and system.

My work exists between the poetic and the precise. I design systems that feel soft and immersive, yet remain grounded in research, intention, and real-world relevance. I am especially drawn to projects that explore sustainability, cultural meaning, human wellbeing, and impact-driven storytelling — translating abstract concepts into experiences that feel both emotionally resonant and structurally clear.

Mycelium Mind

Healing Garden System

Problem: Urban environments often lack spaces that support emotional regulation, reflection, and mental wellbeing.

Concept: A spatial system translating mycelial networks into a non-linear healing environment — encouraging exploration, pause, and interconnected movement.

Impact: Reframes public space as a living system of care, nurturing emotional resilience and community connection.

2026 • Fashion / Cultural Design

Land Meets the Sun

Filipiniana Gown Concept

Problem: Traditional garments are often preserved visually, but not always reinterpreted through evolving identity and contemporary storytelling.

Concept: A material and silhouette exploration translating land, sunlight, and heritage into a contemporary Filipiniana through woven textures and symbolic transition.

Impact: Creates a bridge between cultural preservation and modern expression, allowing identity to evolve while remaining rooted.

2026 • Exhibit / Installation Design

Unextinguished

Light Exhibit for Persian Women

Problem: Stories of resilience and resistance often lack spaces where they can be felt collectively and emotionally.

Concept: An immersive light-based system using atmosphere, layering, and spatial storytelling to embody voice, remembrance, and presence.

Impact: Transforms advocacy into an embodied experience, creating space for visibility, empathy, and collective reflection.
